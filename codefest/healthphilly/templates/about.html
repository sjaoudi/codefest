About

HealthPhilly

The need for a single source of all the available resources for sexual health and family planning became clear to us through data provided by Open Data Philly. This was important information for everyone in the Philly area to know, and yet we realized that knowing all of your options may take a couple google searches. We know people are short on time, but that shouldn't result in risky decisions. We created HealthPhilly at Philly Codefest 2016 to help ensure that Philadelphians make informed decisions and take control of their reproductive health and overall well-being. Educated decisions will lead to happy people, happy families, and happy communities. We hope that we can make a difference with this web app!
